I've read and written poems and my favourite ones' renditions since a very young age. Not all are good, some are purely for the satisfaction of producing a senseless poem I can sing along with (I enjoy rhyming my words). Mostly, I try for my words to reflect how I feel. Also, the accompanying unimpressive photographs are clicked by yours truly :)

I can't thank you enough for stopping by,

For soon our rendezvous will end in goodbye,

Do you want to know me or do you already?

There isn't much here, perhaps a part of who I be.

You still haven't made yourself scarce, have you,

I'd more recommend appreciating else surrounding view,

And if you're continuing not heeding my word,

You're making me conscious, yes that way I'm weird,

We're now sizeable number of lines in,

You'll probably learn with me to see through my grin,

You'll know I value this time and effort of yours,

And that you haven't yet gone to a different course,

Would I henceforth reveal my insecurities,

Or rather skip through the unpleasantries,

Oh how I'll try and serve you nice

While my virtue fights a daily lost battle with vice

Honest, an insignificant monstrosity am I

And rare it's that till now you were here to stay by

Why you'd ask, and I'd share a secret

I sin to brew faults, struggling to together keep

And feed on the bitter words' taste it'd leave

Hey look, here we've gotten this far,

You're repulsed, or mutually interested we are

Though I can't peer through the screen to see,

I'd truly wait to catch up over my coffee.

While we converse what you like or not,

A word for a word, a thought for a thought.

Together we can share laughs and oft a tear,

And I'll grow attached hoping you'll stay near,

We may rejoice in our eternal space,

Which we'll occupy, though it isn't for us to grace,

For I'm an insignificant monstrosity you see,

But aren't you one too, so shall we?



Infinite gratitude for consideration extended,
