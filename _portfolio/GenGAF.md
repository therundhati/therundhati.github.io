This is Animesh's and my contribution to the Artists for Climate Initiative by TED. <br\>
Generation Give a F***: The most hopeful aspect of the future of our planet, for us, is the generation that cares and believes in action. Benevolence beyond boundaries, passion to act across ages and races, and the belief that every bit makes a difference is what characterises our generation. With climate crisis ringing big and loud, the generation joins hands to topple fear with faith. We have rockstars like Greta and Malala who bring others with them and personify the kind of difference one voice can make.
