Our (Animesh and mine) entry to the [Science & Technology Innovation Policy 2021](https://thesciencepolicyforum.org/ideathon-result-announcement/) won the first position and has been chosen as an addition to the policy draft by the Office of the Principal Scientific Advisor, Government of India. <br \>

VISION 2040: Mother India binds together the elements of our thoughts and brings to life our Technikalyug 2040. With the hands each denoting our ideas and VR headset enabling sight before unimagined, the female figure also stands for empowerment of all segments of society with special focus on women, the LGBTQ community, differently abled and minorities in the quest for a better nation. Education, food, access to exploration and platforms to present ideas are fundamental to citizens today and perennial to citizens in 2040. This enables advancement in different fields of science and integration of technology to make lives easier and the world a better place to live. International cooperation and connectivity of remotest places in India to the world is brought about by the efforts of the government and citizens. The idea of sustainability spearheads this development, and the indicator of growth is more than monetary- human development, acceptance and inclusion. India achieves milestones very literally and visually out of this world. A happier, healthier, more informed, sensitised and scientifically aware community is thus engendered. <br/>
