Sonnet 43 ~reimagined~

Here's a rendition of Ms Elizabeth Barrett Browning's classic reimagined in my words for my friend.

I love thee to the depth and breadth and height,

For a hello on our day 1 and then never leaving my side,

For the best and together the worst of days.

I love thee more than one ever says,

Still in awe (and intimidated) by your brilliance,

Looking forward to more while smiling in reminiscence

You add colours and light on my days darkest

And lift me to aim and rise farthest

I love thee purely, as the songs often sing

I love thee in the warmth you diffuse

For all the affection and delight you bring

You're my inspiration and my muse.

I love thee with a love I learnt from you

With all my heart, I love thee with my breath,

Smiles, tears of my life; and if I had to choose one to be close to,

I'd choose you over and over till part do us death.
