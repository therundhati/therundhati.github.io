Poetry

All Things Fright and Beautiful

How do I deal with the fears I face inside and outside my head? Am I really brave enough to overcome them? Is overcoming the fears bravey, or is it getting stuff done despite them? Honestly, I'll never know. Here's my coping mchanism and personal pep talk- it's okay to be scared and amazing to take a step towards them, even more amazing than a step possibly against them :)

Fear, an emotion, overwhelmingly strong,

Clouding priorities, goals, dreams and right and wrong.

Uproot and face your fears, they say,

But is it really so easy to thwart what I wake up to each day?

Do ambition and passion drive my actions, or is it fear, really,

The fear of failure or that of everyone ahead of me.

The fear of disappointment, of shattering expectations,

Pushing me more than any promise of reward or motivations.

The road is lonely, difficult, full of obstacles, hunger and thirst.

So owing to my pre judgement I hesitate to take even the step first.

And thus, fear knowlingly feeds judgements, superstitions, insecurities,

Drawing a line on what I believe are my own possibilities.

I have not been caged, still there is fear’s spectre on my head,

Fear kills more dreams than failure ever will, it’s said.



It had never been easy, it had never been fair,

But what counts is the courage to dream, do and dare.

And it’s not always about the big decisions I make,

It’s about what I pursue and what chances I willingly take.

My biggest fears have carried my greatest growth, experiences, strife.

Fear never did fade, but some things were more important in life.

I have had to take this call whenever,

Fear feels temporary, recurring yet regrets last forever.

Only when the will to prove myself triumphs and clears the doubts’ pall,

The hope that it will be better and I will do better wins above all,

Faith and belief stand stronger than the monsters of my mind,

And greater guts, grit and glory I can find.

Perseverance and actions will keep the rest at bay,

Fear stands insignificant and for success clears the way.

And maybe one day I’ll be able to face and walk towards sunshine,

That’s the day when all my shadows will finally fall behind.
